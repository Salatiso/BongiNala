Our Story: The Heart of BongiNala

BongiNala is far more than a logistics provider; it's a living legacy, forged from the unbreakable bonds of family, an unwavering dedication to service, and a deep-seated understanding of the African continent's pulse. Named in heartfelt tribute to his children, Bonga and Nala, our founder, a proud Zulu father and visionary leader, established BongiNala to build bridges of commerce and opportunity across Southern Africa.

Despite a distinguished career as an academic and COO in a major South African corporate, his inherent passion for logistics and enterprise development fueled the creation of BongiNala. From its strategic base in Newcastle and operational hub in Johannesburg, we've burgeoned into a formidable presence in the transport industry. We proudly serve esteemed clients like Cashbuild and Builders Warehouse, transporting vital commodities such as steel and cement across South Africa and into neighboring sub-Saharan nations.

"At BongiNala, we don't just move cargo; we build connections. We are driven by the spirit of 'Ubuntu' – I am because we are – fostering trust, reliability, and a profound commitment to the communities we serve and the businesses we empower."

Our commitment is to uphold the zenith of service standards, safety protocols, and operational efficiency, ensuring your cargo's journey is secure, timely, and contributes to your success.

Trucking Resources Hub

Key legal & operational insights for trucking in Southern Africa. For general guidance only; consult legal experts for specific advice.

South Africa

Driver Requirements:

  • Valid PrDP; Min. age (often 25 for heavy); Medical cert.; Sober habits.

Load Requirements:

  • Adherence to National Road Traffic Act (dimensions/mass); Load securing (SANS); Abnormal load permits; Hazchem regs.

Inspections & Licenses:

  • COF for commercial vehicles; Company Operator Card; Vehicle licenses.

Cross-Border:

  • C-BRTA permits; Customs docs (SAD 500); TIP for foreign vehicles.

Insurance & Medical:

  • Compulsory 3rd party; GIT insurance vital; Driver medical aid/workmen's comp.

Botswana

Driver Requirements:

  • Valid license (equiv. Botswana Class EC); PrDP or equiv.; Medical fitness.

Load Requirements:

  • Compliance with Road Traffic Act; Secure loading; Permits for abnormal/hazardous loads.

Inspections & Licenses:

  • Vehicle roadworthiness cert.; Transport permits (Dept. of Road Transport & Safety).

Cross-Border:

  • Bilateral/SADC agreements; Customs (BURS); TIPs/Carnets.

Insurance & Medical:

  • Motor 3rd party (Botswana-specific may be needed); GIT insurance.

Zimbabwe

Driver Requirements:

  • Valid license (Class 2 heavy); Defensive Driving Cert.; Medical exam cert.

Load Requirements:

  • Adherence to axle load/dimensions; Secure cargo; Abnormal load permits (Min. of Transport).

Inspections & Licenses:

  • COF (VID); Operator's License.

Cross-Border:

  • COMESA/SADC permits; Customs (ZIMRA); Carbon Tax, Road Access Fees; CVG.

Insurance & Medical:

  • Statutory motor insurance; GIT insurance.

Mozambique

Driver Requirements:

  • Valid Mozambican/SADC license (heavy); Professional driving quals.

Load Requirements:

  • Compliance with Código de Estrada (weight/dimensions); Secure loading; Oversized cargo permits.

Inspections & Licenses:

  • Vehicle inspection cert. (Inspeção Periódica); Transport license ("Alvará de Transporte").

Cross-Border:

  • Bilateral agreements, SADC protocols; Customs (Autoridade Tributária); Local 3rd party insurance; Tolls/levies.

Insurance & Medical:

  • Mandatory 3rd party (Mozambican); GIT insurance.
